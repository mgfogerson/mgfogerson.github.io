    Latitude vs. Weather

    This project seeks to understand the connection between latitude and weather. To do this, observations from 655 cities were charted, measuring 4 different weather effects. To see charts showing the relationship between latitude and cloudiness, humidity, wind speed or temperature, click the charts on the sidebar. Each chart also contains an explanation of any noticed trends. The full table of data, as well as a page to directly compare charts, can be accessed with the top navbar.
